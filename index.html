Success Criteria

A good wedding plan is not busy. It is clear, early, and kind to everyone involved.

faith

Christian ceremony, officiant, procession, vows, worship tone, and family honour are prepared early.

culture

Nigerian food, attire preferences, family travel, music, and reception flow are planned intentionally.

guest care

Invites, RSVP, transport, photos, venue comfort, and timing are clear for local and overseas guests.
